THE BEAST

One night she had dream about the beast.She was in the forest plucking fruits and she realised a huge shadow was beneath her. She was frightened and fear rose in her body.She looked closely at the shadow beneath her and she saw a musculine figure. On the shadow she also saw two horns and a tail.She ran with all her energy she had on her body. She reached on a cliff as she saw the shadow coming closer to her.The fruits that were on her basket were;

  1. Mango
  2. Orange
  3. Apple
  4. Passion

She dropped them since they were getting bulky to continue running with them. She jumped over a cliff as she was going to hit the ground she waked up with a loud scream. Its when she realised it was only just a dream.
